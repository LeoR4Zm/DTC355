Stir-fried Tomato and Scrambled Eggs

A quick, comforting Chinese classic that balances savory eggs with sweet-tart tomatoes. I chose this dish because it’s simple to cook, nutritious, and perfect with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2–3 medium ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 2 tsp cooking oil
  • 1–2 tsp sugar (to taste)
  • ½–1 tsp salt (to taste)
  • ¼ tsp white pepper (optional)
  • 1 tsp light soy sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 1 scallion, sliced (for garnish)
  • Optional additions:
    • 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tsp water (for thickened sauce)
    • ½ tsp grated ginger or 1 garlic clove, minced

Directions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, beat well, and season with a pinch of salt.
  2. Heat a nonstick pan with oil, scramble eggs until just set, then remove from pan.
  3. Add tomatoes to the pan, stir-fry until they soften and release juice.
  4. Season with sugar, salt, and optional soy sauce/white pepper. Add cornstarch slurry if using.
  5. Return eggs to the pan and stir gently until combined. Drizzle with sesame oil.
  6. Garnish with scallions and serve hot over rice.
